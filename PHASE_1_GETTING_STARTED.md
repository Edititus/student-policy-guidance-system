## 🚨 Critical Success Factors

### What Can Derail Phase 1:

1. **Ethics approval delay** → Submit early (Week 5), follow up weekly
2. **Can't recruit participants** → Start with your network, use snowball sampling
3. **Poor audio quality** → Test equipment, use backup, choose quiet locations
4. **Incomplete transcripts** → Transcribe within 48 hours while fresh
5. **Analysis paralysis** → Code as you go, don't wait till all data collected

### Risk Mitigation:

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Ethics delay | High | High | Submit early + use pilot interviews |
| Equipment failure | Medium | High | Always use backup recorder |
| Low participation | Medium | Medium | Offer incentives + convenient times |
| Partner refusal | Medium | Low | Focus on Veritas + public documents |
| Time overrun | High | Medium | Block full days, don't spread out |
